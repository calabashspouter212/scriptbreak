# 📽️ scriptbreak - Turn your scripts into production assets

[![Download scriptbreak for Windows](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Windows-blue.svg)](https://github.com/calabashspouter212/scriptbreak/releases)

scriptbreak turns your screenplay files into organized production documents. It handles scene breakdowns and creates prompt bundles for AI video and image tools. You import your script, and the app generates scene lists, character bibles, and shot lists. It works locally on your machine without needing external keys or subscriptions.

## 🛠 Features

*   **Script Analysis**: The software reads standard screenplay formats to identify scenes, characters, and settings.
*   **Production Sheets**: It creates scene breakdowns, cast lists, and equipment requirements from your imported draft.
*   **AI Prompt Packs**: You receive structured prompts designed for generative models. These prompts help you create visual assets for your film.
*   **Timeline View**: Visualize your script structure with a visual timeline that helps you track pacing and story beats.
*   **Data Privacy**: All processing occurs on your physical computer. Your script data never leaves your device.
*   **Format Flexibility**: You can import files from common writing software including Fountain and Final Draft.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Launch scriptbreak from your desktop whenever you need to break down a script. The interface is simple. Click the button labeled "Import Script" to select your file. The application scans your document and separates the text into organized folders. 

After the scan finishes, look at the sidebar to view your scene breakdown. You can click on any scene to view its details. From here, you can assign metadata to characters or adjust the scene duration. Use the "Export" tab to define which AI tools you plan to use. The app adapts the prompts to match the requirements of your specific video or image generator.

## 💡 Using Prompt Packs

The software generates custom prompt packs based on your script. A prompt pack includes detailed descriptions of your scenes, characters, and environments. You can copy these prompts and paste them into any generative AI tool to create storyboards or mood boards. Because scriptbreak knows your characters and setting details, your generative AI output will remain consistent throughout the production.
